Your logo will be made in the USA by Joshua Blankenship, who has lovingly crafted thousands of logos for clients big and small for decades. It will not be made with AI.

There’s no back and forth, so you can focus on doing what you do best (running your business) and we can focus on what we do best (making you a professional logo at a great price in a week).

Easy as 1 2 3

  1. Buy a ticket. When you’re ready to redeem it, email logoland.biz at gmail and we’ll get started.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of businesses can LogoLand help?
We’re aiming to help small businesses, non-profits, podcasters, and other moms and pops who want a professional logo, but can’t typically afford working with a professional designer.
How do I know LogoLand is a good fit for my business?
We design simple, typography-centric logos and minimalist geometric symbols. If you’re looking for fancy calligraphy, hand drawn text, or an illustrated character, we’re not a good fit. If you’re after a solid logo to identify your business — sturdy stuff that would look good on everything from the side of an 18-wheeler to a business card — we’re your people.
What if I don’t like my logo, man?
That would be a turbo bummer, but we’ve got a pretty great track record of happy clients. Trust the process, trust the designer. We want you to love it. If you like the work above, you’re gonna like what we make for you.
How many revisions are included?
You’re not paying for endless rounds of revisions — we gotta eat! We’ll include one revision to color, composition, or typography. We reserve the right to bill any requests beyond that revision at an hourly rate.
What’s your refund policy?
There are no refunds after you purchase LogoLand’s service.
Who do you think you are, what with your affordable logos and such?!
We’re LogoLand, a service from Joshua Blankenship, an independent designer in Greenville, SC.
$1,000 seems like way too much for a logo.
First, that’s not a question. Secondly, cool — it’s your money. We wish you well.
$1,000 seems like way too little for a logo?
Probably. But we’re just naive optimistic enough to believe we can deliver a good product at this price point. It’s not a full brand identity, and it’s not a full-service agency experience; it’s a professional logo at a great price in a week.
My business doesn’t have a name yet, is that included?
No can do, amigo. This is a logo service, not a naming service. If you need help with your business name, brand strategy, brand identity, etc. see the next question...
Do you offer more than just the logo service?
We do! Or more specifically, Warm is a brand design studio in SC/VA. If you know you need a comprehensive design experience, an ongoing design partner, or you’re not quite sure what you need, please reach out.
